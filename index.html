What we do

We build specific, opinionated things for people who are tired of generic ones.

Ribbescobb Labs is an indie app studio. We make tools that solve real problems in ways that amuse us first and — with any luck — work for you too. Every app we build starts the same way: something in our own life needed to exist, and nothing on the App Store was quite right.

The markets we play in are big and crowded. We are not. We find the corners those bigger players overlook, and we build something worth finding.
