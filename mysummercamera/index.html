What it is

Hold your phone sideways. The volume button is the shutter. Wind the film with your thumb. You get 12 shots.

Every photo comes out the way film came out: a little warm, a little grainy, blacks that never quite reach black. Once in a while the film door leaks and a streak of orange light walks across your shot. We are not going to apologize for that.

There is no feed. There are no likes. There is no filter picker, because the film is the filter and you already bought the camera.

The prints

Permanent. Like consequences.

The film look bakes into every shot at the moment you take it. Not a preview. Not an edit you can undo. What lands in your camera roll is the print, and the print is what happened.
